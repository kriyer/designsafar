About
Design
SAFAR

[suh-fahr]/ səˈfɑr

SAFAR means journey, the pursuit of discovery, connection, and meaning.
A service to support your Design Journey.

At the center of every journey is Hospitality: the meeting point of host, guest, and place. Design SAFAR helps owners and development teams shape that meeting point, bringing strategy and experience together to create places of distinction, relevance, and long-term value.

We are committed to delivering exceptional Hospitality environments.

Design SAFAR brings the insight of Hospitality at its best, paired with the perspective of a trusted advisor. Led by Kavitha Iyer, we offer current intelligence on guest expectations, design shifts, materials, furnishings, and fabrication, translating that knowledge into relevant solutions with creativity.

Kavitha Iyer

Kavitha Iyer is an artist, architect, and brand strategist who shapes environments with clarity, purpose, and care. She believes design should elevate human experience while creating lasting value for brands and owners. With more than two decades of leadership across leading hospitality organizations, she brings a rare ability to align guest connection, brand intent, and commercial performance.

Approach

Services

Early Clarity

  • Project feasibility analysis for site planning, phasing plan, and space planning.
  • Building tailored design narratives to support compelling deal strategy.
  • Scope assessment, PIPs and phasing.
  • Creative direction, concept development, and visualization to define design intent.
  • Holistic cost estimation to build and operate.

Cohesive Process

  • Engagement across Architecture, interiors, sub-consultant, procurement and construction teams.
  • Liaison to streamline reviews between Brand, Hotel management and consultants.
  • Refining designs at each phase with a focus on budget discipline and value.
  • Materials, finish, and fabrication insights to ensure durability and cost effectiveness.

Market Confident

  • Advisor on local competition and differentiation recommendations.
  • Brand design partnerships to develop property specific collateral development.
  • Pre-opening set up inclusive of staging, styling, and accessorizing.
  • Photoshoot direction and art coordination.
